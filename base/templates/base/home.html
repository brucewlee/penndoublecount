What does this Program do?

PennDoubleCount helps Penn undergraduates identify all sorts of "double counting" major requirements.

You can use this program to 1. see all the double counting major requirements (for dual degree students) 2. check AP credit that applies to you (for single/dual degree students) 3. do both.
The requirement lists were self-created by web-scraping SAS and SEAS websites. Wharton and Nursing school pages seem to have different formattings. They seem to have very school-specific major requirements so I didn't extract their data for now.

I'll gradually improve PennDoubleCount so that it can be useful to Wharton and Nursing students too.
- How to Use -

1. Choose your degree(s) in the box below (choose "none" for degree B if you're a single degree student)

2. Choose your AP Score(s) in the box below (leave blank if you don't have one)

3. Check your major requirements for graduation and the calculation below
