What does this Program do?

This simple program is designed for Penn dual degree students in SAS and SEAS. It automatically returns the "double counting" major requirements between the two majors that you are pursuing. Save some time :)

Inside, all the program doing is just returning the duplicate items in two major requirement lists that you choose. The lists were self-created by web-scraping SAS and SEAS websites. Wharton and Nursing school pages seem to have different formattings. They seem to have umm... very school-specific major requirements so I didn't extract their data.

Next time, I'll try to make something useful to Wharton and Nursing students too.
